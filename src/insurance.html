Business Life Insurance

This is crucial for a business owner as it removes the uncertanity for a business after their passing. We can help find you cover for both your business and/or your family.

Key Person - Should an instrumental person in a business pass away or fall ill, this cover will insure itself from any financial losses

Relevant Life - Paid for by an employer, this cover will be paid to an employee's family should the employee pass away. This cover is usually an attractive part of a work package.

Business Loan Protection - This cover is used to pay off loans or debts should a key individual within the business pass away.
